<section id="what" class="panel" aria-labelledby="what-title">
  <header>
    <p class="eyebrow">What</p>
    <h2 id="what-title">What ESA BIC Centro+ delivers</h2>
  </header>
  <p>
    ESA BIC Centro+ is the Portuguese entry point to the ESA Business Incubation programme. We provide direct funding,
    curated technical mentoring, and regional business support so that space-derived technologies can scale both in orbit and on Earth.
  </p>
  <div class="stat-grid">
    <div>
      <h3>Funding and runway</h3>
      <p>Up to €60 k non-equity ESA seed funding, plus access to follow-on pilots with industrial partners.</p>
    </div>
    <div>
      <h3>Technical bridges</h3>
      <p>Space-grade labs and testing facilities at Instituto Pedro Nunes keep R&amp;D practical.</p>
    </div>
    <div>
      <h3>Market intelligence</h3>
      <p>Mentors, investors, and corporate partners from Coimbra and Europe help founders prove demand.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section id="why" class="panel" aria-labelledby="why-title">
  <header>
    <p class="eyebrow">Why</p>
    <h2 id="why-title">Why founders choose Centro+</h2>
  </header>
  <p>
    We keep interaction lean: regular coaching, twice-monthly check-ins, and clearly defined milestones so entrepreneurs keep
    control of their roadmap while ESA quality standards are met.
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>One stable cohort rhythm ensures every team receives equal attention and transparent evaluation.</li>
    <li>Portuguese and European regulators, as well as corporate partners, convene here so validation paths stay short.</li>
    <li>Coimbra universities, innovation hubs, and industry keep domain expertise in sensors, robotics, telecoms, and life sciences close at hand.</li>
    <li>The programme merges ESA heritage with the IPN innovation engine, so teams benefit from both networks.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section id="for-whom" class="panel" aria-labelledby="for-whom-title">
  <header>
    <p class="eyebrow">For whom</p>
    <h2 id="for-whom-title">Who can be supported</h2>
  </header>
  <p>
    Centro+ admits dedicated founding teams that already have technical proof of concept and want to commercialise space-enabled applications.
    Preference is given to teams that can show both a non-space market (Earth) and a credible space path.
  </p>
  <dl class="definition-list">
    <dt>Stages</dt>
    <dd>Pre-seed and seed teams with working prototypes and early validation.</dd>
    <dt>Geography</dt>
    <dd>Portugal-based teams or ESA member state founders committed to operating from Coimbra for the programme duration.</dd>
    <dt>Focus areas</dt>
    <dd>Earth observation analytics, orbital and ground robotics, telecommunications, sustainable engineering, and dual-use sensors.</dd>
  </dl>
</section>

<section id="how" class="panel" aria-labelledby="how-title">
  <header>
    <p class="eyebrow">How</p>
    <h2 id="how-title">How the programme works</h2>
  </header>
  <p>
    Applications run twice a year. Each cohort receives structured technical coaching, market-building workshops, and ESA-grade due diligence.
    ESA BIC Centro+ measures progress through transparent milestones so every team knows what is expected next.
  </p>
  <ol>
    <li>
      Draft a short dossier outlining the technology, market, and team. Use ESA’s template and keep the story factual.
    </li>
    <li>
      Submit the dossier through the ESA BIC portal so the ESA central team can match the request with the Centro+ focus areas.
    </li>
    <li>
      The selection panel of ESA, IPN, and partners reviews technical merit, market pull, and sustainability for the reference period.
    </li>
    <li>
      Selected teams sign the ESA BIC contract, receive the funding disbursement path, and join Centro+ for up to 36 months of support.
    </li>
    <li>
      Regular reviews keep founders accountable to milestones, while Centro+ curates opportunities with investors and corporates.
    </li>
  </ol>
